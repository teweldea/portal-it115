About Me (Change made in main)

Hey, my name is Tewelde Abraha.
I'm an aspiring UX designer studying application development at Seattle Central College via Year Up. Ethical design with an emphasis of inclusion is where my passion resides. I love working on projects with teams who are diligent and intentional. I'm currently learning and developing technical and professional skills as a student in the Year Up program. I love my Eritrean heritage, which in turn guides my advocacy for all disenfranchised communities.
